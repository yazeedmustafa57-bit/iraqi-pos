# Play Store Submission Guide

## Step 1: Create Google Play Console Account
1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Pay $25 USD (one-time fee)
4. Verify your identity with ID
5. Wait 1-2 days for Google approval

## Step 2: Create New App
1. In Play Console, click "Create App"
2. Fill in:
   - App name: "Kashir POS" (English) / "كاشير POS" (Arabic)
   - Default language: Arabic
   - App type: App
   - Free: Yes
3. Click "Create"

## Step 3: Store Listing
1. Go to "Store presence" → "Main store listing"
2. Fill in:
   - App name: كاشير POS
   - Short description: (from store-listing.md)
   - Full description: (from store-listing.md)
3. Upload screenshots (see Step 4)
4. Upload feature graphic (1024x500)
5. Upload app icon (512x512)
6. Save

## Step 4: Screenshots
You need at least 2 screenshots. Recommended 4-6:

### How to take screenshots:
1. Open Kashir POS on your phone/tablet
2. Go to each screen
3. Press Power + Volume Down (simultaneously)
4. Screenshot is saved to Gallery

### Screenshots needed:
1. Login screen
2. Product catalog
3. Cart + payment
4. Barcode scanner
5. Daily summary
6. Settings

### Screenshot requirements:
- Minimum: 320px wide
- Maximum: 3840px wide
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 9:16
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- File size: Max 15MB each

## Step 5: Content Rating
1. Go to "Store presence" → "Content rating"
2. Fill questionnaire:
   - Violence: None
   - Sexual content: None
   - Language: None
   - Controlled substances: None
   - etc.
3. Submit for rating

## Step 6: Privacy Policy
1. Host privacy policy on GitHub Pages
2. Go to "Store presence" → "Privacy policy"
3. Enter URL: https://yazeed5353.github.io/iraqi-pos/privacy-policy/
4. Save

## Step 7: Data Safety
1. Go to "Store presence" → "Data safety"
2. Fill form (see data-safety.md)
3. Submit

## Step 8: App Content
1. Go to "Policy" → "App content"
2. Fill all required sections:
   - Privacy policy
   - Data safety
   - Content rating
   - Target audience
3. Submit

## Step 9: Release
1. Go to "Release" → "Production"
2. Click "Create new release"
3. Upload AAB file (from EAS build)
4. Add release notes
5. Review and roll out

## Step 10: Review
- Google reviews your app (1-3 days)
- If approved → App is live!
- If rejected → Fix issues and resubmit
