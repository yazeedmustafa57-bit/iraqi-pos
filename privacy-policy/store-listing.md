## Full Description (English):

Kashir POS is a professional point of sale system designed specifically for Iraqi shops and stores. Whether you run a small grocery store, a supermarket, or any retail business, Kashir POS makes managing your sales easy and efficient.

**KEY FEATURES:**

🛒 **Product Management**
- Add and manage your products with prices in Iraqi Dinar (IQD)
- Organize products by categories (Food, Drinks, Snacks, Household, Electronics)
- Set stock quantities and track inventory
- Add barcodes to products for quick scanning

📱 **Barcode Scanner**
- Use your phone camera to scan product barcodes
- Instantly find products and add them to cart
- Beep sound confirmation on successful scan
- Manual barcode input option

💳 **Multiple Payment Methods**
- Cash (with change calculation)
- ZainCash
- FastPay
- AsiaHawala
- FIB Bank
- Credit Card
- Offline payment queue for electronic methods

🖨️ **Bluetooth Thermal Printer**
- Connect to any BLE thermal receipt printer
- Automatic receipt printing after payment
- Custom receipt with your shop name
- Test print function

📊 **Daily Summary**
- Track total sales and transactions
- Sales breakdown by payment method
- Transaction history for the day
- Export-ready data

🌐 **Multi-Language Support**
- Arabic (Full RTL support)
- Kurdish (Sorani)
- English

🔐 **Security**
- PIN-based login system
- Shop owner registration
- Secure local data storage

📶 **Offline Ready**
- Works without internet connection
- Automatic sync when connection returns
- All data stored locally on your device

**PERMISSIONS:**
- Camera: For barcode scanning
- Bluetooth: For receipt printer connection
- Internet: For electronic payment sync

**SUPPORT:**
Email: support@iraqi-pos.com

Kashir POS - Your trusted Iraqi POS system 🇮🇶
